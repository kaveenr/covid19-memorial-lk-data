Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three deaths caused by Covid-19
virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 557 by now.

1. The deceased is a 79 year old male resident from Passara. He died on
24.03.2021 while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Badulla. The
cause of death is mentioned as Ischemic Heart Disease complicated with
COVID infection.

2. The deceased is a 64 year old male resident from Niriella. He was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at Teaching
Hospital Rathnapura and transferred to Base Hospital Homagama where he
died on 24.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Sepsis,
Hypertension, Diabetes Mellitus, Chronic Kidney Disease and COVID
Infection.

3. The deceased is a 64 year old male resident from Kandy. He was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at a private
hospital and transferred to Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on
24.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as severe COVID Pneumonia
and Acute respiratory distress syndrome
