Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed two (02) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 276 by now.

01.The deceased is a 71 year old female from Peliyagoda. She was diagnosed as
infected with Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital,
Colombo to Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where she died on 20.01.2021. The
Cause of death is mentioned as acute diabetes pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 46 year old male from Athurugiriya. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo
to Base Hospital Homagama where he died on 19.01.2021. The cause of
death is multiple organ dysfunctions due to nervous system infection with
Covid pneumonia.
