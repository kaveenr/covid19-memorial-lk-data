The Director General of Health Services confirmed a death caused by Covid-19 virus
infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri
Lanka is 256 by now.

01.The deceased is an 82 year old female from Ethul Kotte. She was diagnosed as
infected with Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo
to IDH Hospital where she died on 16.01.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as an acute heart disease with Covid-19 infection.
