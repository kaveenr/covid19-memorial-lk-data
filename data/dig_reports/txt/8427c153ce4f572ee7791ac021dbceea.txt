11 Covid Deaths Reported

The Director General of Health Services confirmed eleven (11) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today (30). The total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 678 by now.

1. The deceased is a 79 year old female resident in Piltyandala. She died on
30.04.2021 on admission to a private hospital in Colombo. The cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

2. The deceased is a 76 year old male resident in Dehiattakandiya. He died on
30.04.2021 while undergoing treatments in General Hospital Polonnaruwa.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and Severe Heart
Failure.

3. The deceased is a 67 year old female resident in Mathale. She died on
29.04.2021 while undergoing treatments in District General Hospital
Mathale. The Cause of death is mentioned as Covid pulmonary Syndrome.

4. The deceased is an 82 year old male resident in Kalutara. He was
transferred from Sri Jayawardhanapura General Hospital to the Base
Hospital Ingiriya where he died on 30.04.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

5. The deceased is a 53 year old male resident in Wattala. He was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments in National
Hospital Colombo and transferred to the Kothalawala Defense University
Hospital where he died on 29.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as
Covid pneumonia and Respiratory Tract Infection.

6. The deceased is a 51 year old male resident in Divulapitiya. He died on
29.04.2021 in his residence and the cause of death is mentioned as Chronic
Liver disease complicated with Covid 19 infection.
