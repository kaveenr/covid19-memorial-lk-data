Six Covid Death Today

The Director General of Health Services confirmed six (06) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection today (28). The total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Si Lanka is 661
by now.

1. The deceased is a 56 year old male resident in Pallegama. He died on 25.04.2021
while undergoing treatments in Base Hospital Mawanella. The cause of death is
mentioned as COVID Pneumonia.

2. The deceased is a 45 year old female resident in Weerapokuna. She was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 while undergoing treatments in District General Hospital
Chilaw and transferred to Base Hospital Homagama where she died on 27.04.2021.
The cause of death is mentioned as COVID pneumonia and Sepsis.

3. The deceased is a 72 year old male resident in Haliela. He was transferred from
General Hospital Baudlla to Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on 28.04.2021.
The cause of death is mentioned as acute COVID Pneumonia.

4. The deceased is a 65 year old f female resident in Mawanella. She was transferred
from Base Hospital Mawanella to Kothalawala Defense Hospital and died on
23.04.2021 on the way to Kothalawala Defense Hospital. The cause of death is
mentioned as Heart failure aggravated due to COVID 19 infection.

5. The deceased is a 66 year old male resident in Nittambuwa.He died on 24.04.2021
while undergoing treatments in Base Hospital Wathupitiwala. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid 19 Lung infection.

6. The deceased is a 69 year old male resident in Wattala. He died on 27.04.2021 while
undergoing treatments in Chest Hospital Welisara. The cause of death is mentioned
as Covid pneumonia.
