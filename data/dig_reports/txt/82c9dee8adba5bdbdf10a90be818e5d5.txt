Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three (03) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 194 by now.

01.The deceased is a 90 year old male from Darga Twon. He died on admission
to District Hospital Kaluthara on 24.12.2020. The cause of death is mentioned
as blood poisoning and a heart attack due to high blood pressure with Covid-
19 virus infection.

02. The deceased is an 83 year old male from Theldeniya. He was transferred from
General Hospital Kandy to Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on
28.12.2020. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and an
acute kidney disease.

03.The deceased is a 57 year old female from Kaluthara South. She died on
admission to District Hospital Kaluthara on 22.12.2020 The cause of death is
mentioned as blood poisoning with Covid pneumonia.
