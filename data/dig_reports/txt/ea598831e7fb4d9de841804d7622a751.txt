Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 244

by now.

The deceased is an 82 year old female from Colombo 13. She was diagnosed as infected
with Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo to IDH Hospital
where she died on 10.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as blood poisoning with
multiple organ dysfunction and Covid pneumonia.

The deceased is a 47 year old male from Hanwella. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo to Base Hospital
Homagama where he died on 12.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

The deceased is an 84 year old female from Mathale. She was diagnosed as infected with
Covid-19 virus and transferred from Mathale District Hospital to Base Hospital
Theldeniya where she died on 12.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as poor blood
supply to the heart due to Covid-19 infection.

The deceased is a 65 year old male from Wellampitiya. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo to Base Hospital
Mulleriyawa where he died on 12.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as a stroke
and Covid Pneumonia.
