Media Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three deaths caused by Covid-
19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 232 by now.

01.The deceased is a 62 year old male inmate from Negombo Prison. He was
transferred from Negombo General Hospital to Mulleriyawa Base Hospital
where he died on 08.01.2021.The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

02.The deceased is an 80 year old female from Colombo-14. She was diagnosed
as infected with Covid-19 virus and transferred from Apeksha Hospital
Maharagama to Pimbura Base Hospital where she died on 10.01.2021 The
cause of death is mentioned as a cancer and Covid pneumonia.

03. The deceased is a 64 year old male from Rathnapura. He died on 09.01.2021
while undergoing treatments at Rathnapura Teaching Hospital. The cause of
death is Covid pneumonia and a heart disease.
