Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed a death caused by Covid-19
virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 552 by now.

01.The deceased is a 63 year old female resident from Nallur in Jaffna. She died
on 22.03.2021 while undergoing treatments at Teaching Hospital Jaffna. The

cause of death is mentioned as breathing difficulty, cough, diarrhea and
Covid pneumonia.
