Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 208 by now.

01.The deceased is a 67 year old male from Alayadivembu. He was transferred
from Welikanda Base Hospital to IDH hospital where he died on 01.01.2021.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 91 year old female from Colombo 14. She died on
01.01.2021 while undergoing treatments at IDH hospital. The cause of death
is blood poisoning and Covid pneumonia.

03.The deceased is a 65 year old male from Agalawatta. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid -19 virus at the Kalutara General Hospital and transferred
to Homagama Base Hospital where he died on 31.12.2020. The cause of death
is liver failure and Covid pneumonia.

04. The deceased is a 63 year old male from Darga Town. He died on admission
to the Kalutara General Hospital on 29.12.2020. The cause of death is
mentioned as blood poisoning and heart disease with Covid pneumonia.
