Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 229 by now.

01.The deceased is a 74 year old female from Mount Lavinia. She was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus at Colombo South Teaching
Hospital and died on 07.01.2021 on admission to Homagama Base Hospital.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and severe asthma.

02.The deceased is a 64 year old male from Kaththankudi. He was diagnosed as
infected with covid-19 virus and transferred from Kalmunai Base Hospital to
Homagama Base Hospital where he died on 09.01.2021. The cause of death
is Covid pneumonia.

03.The deceased is a 76 year old male from Wellampitiya. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid-19 virus and transferred from Colombo General Hospital
to Homagama Base Hospital where he died on 09.01.2021. The cause of
death is mentioned as blood poisoning with Covid pneumonia.

04.The deceased is a 58 year old male from Opanayake. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Rathnapura Teaching
Hospital to Homagama Base Hospital where he died on 08.01.2021. The
cause of death is Covid pneumonia.
