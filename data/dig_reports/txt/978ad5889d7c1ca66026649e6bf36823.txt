Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed five (05) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 181 by now.

01.The deceased is a 68 year old male inmate of the Welikada Prison. He
deceased on 17.12.2020 in prison Hospital. The course of death is Covid
pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 55 year old male from Colombo 15. He died on 19.12.2020
at Colombo General Hospital. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia and acute kidney disease.

03.The deceased is a 77 year old male from Darga Town. He died in his
residence on 18.12.2020. The Cause of death is blood poisoning due to Covid
19 infection and heart decease.

04.The deceased is a 63 year old female from Maggona. She was died in her
residence on 19.12.2020. The Cause of death is blood poisoning due to
Covid 19 infection and heart decease with high blood pressure.

05.The deceased is an 83 year old male from Colombo 10. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo
to Base Hospital Homagama where he died on 20.12.2020. The cause of
death is Covid pneumonia.
