Nine Covid Deaths Reported

The Director General of Health Services confirmed nine deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today (01). The total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 687 by now.

1. The deceased is a 65 year old male resident in Karandana. He died on
29.04.2021 while undergoing treatments in Base Hospital Horana. The
cause of death is mentioned as COVID 19 Chest infection and Chronic
Liver Disease.

2. The deceased is a 72 year old male resident in Awissawella. He was
transferred from Kopai Covid Treatment Centre to Teaching Hospital
Jaffna where he died on 30.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as
acute coronary syndrome with heart failure complicated with COVID 19.

3. The deceased is a 75 year old male resident in Ruwanwella. He was
admitted to Base Hospital Wathupitiwala and transferred to Sri
Jayawardhanapura General Hospital. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments there and transferred to the
Base Hospital Hoamagama where he died on 28.04.2021. The cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

4. The deceased is a 66 year old female resident in Dewalagama. She died on
30.04.2021 while undergoing treatments in Base Hospital Homagama. The
cause of death is mentioned as Respiratory failure due to in COVID 19
infection.

5. The deceased is a 79 year old male resident in Mawanella. He was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments in
Base Hospital Warakapola and transferred to the Base Hospital Theldeniya
where he died on 30.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Sever
COVID Pneumonia with the background of sever Chronic Obstructive
Pulmonary Disease, Diabetes Mellitus and Hypertension
