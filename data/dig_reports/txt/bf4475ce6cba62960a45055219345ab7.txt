Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by

Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to

Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 608 by now.

1,

The deceased is a 62 year old male resident in Pannipitiya. He was
transferred from Colombo South Teaching Hospital to Base Hospital
Mulleriyawa where he died on 12.04.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid 19 infection with hepatorenal failure.

. The deceased is a 72 year old female resident in Ellakkala. she was

diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus at Base Hospital Wathupitiwala
and transferred to Base Hospital Minuwangoda where she died on
14.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

. The deceased is a 78 year old male resident in Kapuliyadda. He was

transferred from District Hospital Marassana to National Hospital Kandy
where he died on 15.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia, heart disease and high blood pressure.

. The deceased is a 34 year old male resident in Deniyaya. He was

diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 at Apeksha Hospital Maharagama
and transferred to IDH Hospital where he died on 14.04.2021. The cause
of death is mentioned as Leukemia and Covid pneumonia.
