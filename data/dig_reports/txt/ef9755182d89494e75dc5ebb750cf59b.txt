Travel restrictions imposed to control Covid-19 spread

Two types of travel restrictions have been imposed with effect from today
Wednesday (12) to control Covid 19 spread in the country.

Army Commander, Head of the National Operations Center for Prevention of
Covid 19 outbreak, General Shavendra Silva announced that travel restrictions
will be imposed from 11.00 pm to 4.00 am daily with effect from today (12) to
Monday, May 31.

In addition, islandwide travel restrictions will be in force from 11.00 pm tomorrow
(Thursday 13) to 4.00 am next Monday (17). The Army Commander further states
that there is no impediment to the transportation of essential goods and food items
during this period.
