Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three (03) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 211 by now.

01.The deceased is a 93 year old female from Colombo 13. She died on
30.12.2020 at her residence. The cause of death is mentioned as multiple
complications of Covid-19 infection.

02.The deceased is a male around 70 - 80 of age whose identity has not been
verified. He was found in Maradana police division and died on 30.12.2020.
The cause of death is Covid-19 infection.

03.The deceased is a 76 year old male from Colombo 12. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid -19 virus at a private hospital in Colombo and transferred
to Base Hospital Homagama where he died on 02.01.2021. The cause of death
is Covid pneumonia and complicated kidney infection.
