Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three (03) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 247
by now.

01. The deceased is a 66 year old male from Baththaramulla. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid-19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo to Base Hospital
Homagama where he died on 12.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as acute heart
decease due to Covid-19 virus infection.

02. The deceased is an 81 year old male from Colombo 15. He died on 12.01.2021 while
undergoing treatments at IDH Hospital. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
Pneumonia and acute respiratory tract infection.

03. The deceased is an 89 year old female from Colombo 10. She died on admission to the
General Hospital Colombo on 12.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.
