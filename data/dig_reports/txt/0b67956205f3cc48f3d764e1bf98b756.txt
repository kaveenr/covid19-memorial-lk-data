Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 515 by now.

01.The deceased is a 74 year old male resident from Colombo 14. He died on
08.03.2021 at his residence and the cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 57 year old male resident from Colombo 09. He died on
08.03.2021 on admission to the General Hospital Colombo. The cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and acute diabetes.

03.The deceased is a 23 year old male resident from Alawathugoda. He died
on 08.03.2021 while undergoing treatments at National Hospital Kandy. The
cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and acute blood poisoning.

04.The deceased is a 77 year old female resident from Nugegoda. She was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 while undergoing treatments at Colombo
South Teaching Hospital and transferred to Base Hospital Pimbura where she
died on 10.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and
blood poisoning.
