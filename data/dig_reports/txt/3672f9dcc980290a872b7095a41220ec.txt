Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed three (03) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 433 by now.

01.The deceased is an 83 year old male from Mathale. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Mathale to
Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on 18.02.2021. The cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 73 year old male from Kalagedihena. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 and transferred from Base Hospital Wathupitiwala to
IDH Hospital where he died on 19.02.2021 The cause of death is mentioned
as Covid pneumonia and a bacterial infection.

03.The deceased is a 63 year old male from Nugegoda. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 and transferred from a private Hospital Colombo to
IDH Hospital where he died on 18.02.2021. The cause of death is mentioned
as Covid pneumonia, acute kidney damages, kidney disease, high blood
pressure and acute diabetes.
