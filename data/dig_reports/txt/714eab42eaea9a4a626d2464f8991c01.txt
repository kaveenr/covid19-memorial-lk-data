Press Release

A short while ago, the Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04)
deaths caused by Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of
deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 122 by now.

01.The deceased is a 74 year old male from Colombo 12. He died in his
residence on 29" November 2020. The cause of death is a stroke caused by a
germ infection that occurred with the Covid-19 virus infection.

02.The deceased is a 74 year old female from Kolonnawa. She died on
29.11.2020 in her residence. The cause of death is a bleeding inside brain
with Covid-19 virus infection.

03.The deceased is a 93 year old female from Rajagiriya. She died on
28.11.2020 in her residence. The cause of death is blood poisoning with
Covid infection and a skin infection.

04.The deceased is an 81 year old male from Colombo 10. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 by a private hospital and transferred to Mulleriyawa
base Hospital where he died on 30.11.2020. The cause of death is Covid 19
infection with a chronic lung disease.
