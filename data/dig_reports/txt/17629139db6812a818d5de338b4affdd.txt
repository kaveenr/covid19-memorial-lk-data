34 Covid Deaths Reported

The Director General of Health Services today (18) confirmed 34 deaths that occurred between
the 11" to 18 May due to Covid 19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths
due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 1015 by now.
