Press Release

A short while ago, the Director General of Health Services confirmed three (03)
deaths caused by Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of
deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 140 by now.

01.The deceased is a 98 year old male from Kotte. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid-19 virus and admitted to Homagama Basic Hospital
where he died on 06.12.2020. The cause of death is Covid pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 80 year old male from Kahathuduwa and died on
05.12.2020 at Homagama Basic Hospital. The cause of death is kidney
failure with Covid-19 virus infection.

03.The deceased is a 71 year old male from Maggona. He died in Intensive

Care Unit of Homagama Basic Hospital on 05.12.2020. The cause of death
is Covid pneumonia.
