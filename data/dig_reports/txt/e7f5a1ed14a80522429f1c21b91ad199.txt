Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed five (05) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 176 by now.

01.The deceased is a 71 year old male from Panagoda. He was transferred from
the Base Hospital Pimbura to Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where he died on
18.12.2020. The cause of death is blood poisoning and Covid pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 52 year old male from Colombo 08. He died on 17.12.2020
in his residence. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

03.The deceased is a 71 year old female from Colombo 12. She was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from General Hospital
Colombo to Base Hospital Pimbura where she died on 19.12.2020. The cause
of death is blood poisoning due to Covid 19 infection and a cancer.

04.The deceased is a 44 year old male from Colombo 02. He was transferred
from General Hospital Colombo to Chest Hospital, Welisara where he died
on 19.12.2020. The cause of death is an acute lung infection due to Covid-
19 virus infection.

05.The deceased is a 49 year old male from Bandaragama. He died on admission
to Base Hospital Horana on 19.12.2020. The cause of death is a chest
infection due to Covid 19 virus.
