Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed two (02) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 617 by now.

1. The deceased is a 73 year old female resident in Kelaniya. She died on
16.04.2021 at National Hospital Colombo. The cause of death is mentioned
as COVID Pneumonia, Diabetes, Hypertension, TB and Cerebro vascular
accident.

2. The deceased is a 71 year old male resident in Kurunegala. He died on
16.04.2021 at a private hospital in Colombo. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia, Diabetes and High Hypertension.
