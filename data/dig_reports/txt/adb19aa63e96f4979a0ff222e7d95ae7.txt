36 Covid Deaths Reported

The Director General of Health Services today (19) confirmed 36 deaths that occurred between
the 06" to 19tt May due to Covid 19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths
due to Covid-19 infection in Si Lanka is 1051 by now.
