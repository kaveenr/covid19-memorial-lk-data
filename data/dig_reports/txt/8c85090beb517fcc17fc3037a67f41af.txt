General Shavendra Silva, Commander of the Army, Head of the National Operations Center
for Prevention of Covid 19 outbreak, announces that following 70 GN Divisions and areas in
Batticaloa, Trincomalee, Kurunegala, Gampaha, Rathnapura, Galle, Ampara, Kaluthara,
Mathale and Nuwaraeliya Districts have been designated as isolated areas with effect from
6.00 AM on 17.05.2021 and 36 isolated GN Divisions and areas in Trincomalee, Kauthara,
Batticaloa, Nuwaraeliya, Gampaha, Polonnaruwa and Kurunegala District have been released
from isolation as follows with effect from today.
