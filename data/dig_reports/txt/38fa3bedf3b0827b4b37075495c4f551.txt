Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed eight (08) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 351 by now.

01.The deceased is a 77 year old female from Kochchikade. She was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from District Hospital
Negombo to the IDH Hospital where she died on 06.02.2021. The Cause of
death is mentioned as shock due to blood poisoning, Covid pneumonia and
acute kidney disease.

02.The deceased is a 73 year old female from Kadawatha. She was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Colombo North Teaching
Hospital to the Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where she died on 05.02.2021. The
Cause of death is mentioned as heart disease, Covid pneumonia and acute
kidney disease.

03.The deceased is a 56 year old male from Moratuwa. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Colombo South Teaching
Hospital to the Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where he died on 05.02.2021. The
Cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and a cancer.

04.The deceased is an 83 year old male from Kelaniya. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Chest Hospital Welisara to
the District Hospital Dankotuwa where he died on 06.02.2021. The Cause of
death is mentioned as disorders of the heart and respiratory system due to
Covid infection, a cancer and a complicated liver disease.
