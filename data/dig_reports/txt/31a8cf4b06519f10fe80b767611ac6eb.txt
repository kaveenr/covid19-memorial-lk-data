21 Covid Deaths Reported today

The Director General of Health Services today (16) confirmed 21 deaths due to
Covid 19 virus infection. Out of these deaths 20 occurred between the 11" to
16" May. The 21“ death which was reported today had occurred on 07.02.2021.
Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka
is 962 by now.

01.The deceased is a 65 year old male resident in Imaduwa. He was
transferred from Imaduwa Hospital to the Teaching Hospital Karapitiya.
where he died on 11.05.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
19 pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 72 year old male resident in Muruthgahamula. He died
on 15.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in Base Hospital Gampola.
The cause of death is mentioned as Pneumonia due to COVID 19
infection.

03.The deceased is a 58 year old male resident in Anuradhapura. He died on
14.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in a private hospital in Colombo.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid Pneumonia, Chronic kidney
disease, and Diabetes.

04.The deceased is a 68 year old male resident in Moratuwa. He died on
16.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in a private hospital in Colombo.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid Pneumonia, Chronic Liver cell
disease and Chronic kidney disease.

05.The deceased is a 70 year old male resident in Meerigama. He died on
12.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in a National Hospital Colombo.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid Pneumonia, Hypertension,
Diabetes and Heart disease.
