The Director General of Health Services confirmed eight (08) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 264
by now.

January 13 (01

01. The deceased is a 63 year old female from Kolonnawa. She died on 13.01.2021 while
undergoing treatments at General Hospital Colombo. The cause of death is mentioned as
Covid pneumonia.

January 14 01)
02. The deceased is a 75 year old male from Borella. He was diagnosed as infected with Covid-

19 virus and transferred from a private Hospital in Colombo to Base Hospital Mulleriyawa
where he died on 14.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as_ high blood pressure and
Covid pneumonia.

January 15 (01

03. The deceased is a 27 year old male from Udupussellawa. He was_ transferred from Base
Hospital Udupussellawa to District General Hospital Nuwara Eliya where he died on
15.01.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid-19 infection and Lung Infection.

January 16 (02
04. The deceased is an 87 year old male from Kegalle. He died on 16.01.2021 while undergoing

treatments at Homagama Base Hospital. The cause of death is mentioned as a heart disease
and acute Bronchitis due to Covid-19 virus infection.

05. The deceased is a 72 year old male from Dehiwala. He was diagnosed as infected with Covid
19 virus and transferred from a private hospital in Colombo to Base Hospital Homagama
where he died on 16.01.2021. The cause of death is Covid pneumonia and a heart disease.
