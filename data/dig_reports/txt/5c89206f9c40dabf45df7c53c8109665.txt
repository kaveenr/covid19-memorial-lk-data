Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed six (06) deaths caused by Covid-
19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 544 by now.

01.The deceased is a 70 year old male resident from Polgahawela. He was
transferred from Teaching Hospital Kurunegala to General Hospital Kandy
where he died on 18.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

02. The deceased is a 79 year old female resident from Suduhumpola. She died on
17.03.2021 while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Kandy. The cause
of death is mentioned as a heart disease and Covid pneumonia.

03.The deceased is an 83 year old female resident from Wattala. She died on
19.02.2021 at her residence. The cause of death is mentioned as acute Covid
infection.

04.The deceased is a 96 year old female resident from Pitakotte. She died on
14.03.2021 at her residence. The cause of death is mentioned as acute Covid
infection.

05.The deceased is a 72 year old male resident from Ganemulla. He died on
18.02.2021 at his residence and the cause of death is mentioned as acute Covid
infection acute diabetes and high blood pressure.

06. The deceased is a 73 year old female resident from Chilawathurai. She died on
10.02.2021 at her residence. The cause of death is mentioned as acute Covid
infection.
