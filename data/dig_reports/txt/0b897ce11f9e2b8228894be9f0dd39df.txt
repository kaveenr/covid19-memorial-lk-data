Press Release
The Director General of Health Services confirmed seven (07) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 323
by now.
01. The deceased is a 75 year old female from Wattala. She was transferred from Colombo
North Teaching Hospital to the Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where she died on 31.01.2021.
The cause of death is mentioned as a kidney disease complicated by Covid 19 infection and
a stroke due to blood poisoning.

02. The deceased is a 69 year old male from Nugegoda. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid 19 and transferred from Apeksha Hospital Maharagama to the base hospital
Homagama where he died on 01.02.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia and a cancer.

03. The deceased is a 72 year old male from Colombo 02. He was diagnosed as infected with
Covid-19 virus and transferred from a private hospital in Colombo to the Base hospital
Homagama where he died on 31.01.2021. The Cause of death is mentioned as an acute lung
infection with Covid 19 infection and a heart attack.

04. The deceased is a 69 year old male from Udugampola. He died on 01.02.2021 on admission
to District Hospital, Gampaha. The cause of deathis mentioned as a heart attack with Covid-
19 infection.

05. The deceased is a 39 year old male from Polonnaruwa. He died on 30.01.2021 at his
tesidence. The cause of death is mentioned as multiple organ dysfunctions with blood
poisoning, bronchopneumonia with Covid-19 infection and a kidney infection.

06. The deceased is a 73 year old female from Madawala. She died on 01.02.2021 while
undergoing treatments at Base Hospital Theldeniya. The cause of death is mentioned as
Covid pneumonia and heart failure.

07. The deceased is a 77 year old male from Gelioya. He was transferred from Teaching
Hospital Peradeniya to the Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on 01.02.2021. The
cause of death is mentioned as blood poisoning with Covid pneumonia, acute diabetes and
a kidney disease.
