20 Covid Deaths Reported today

The Director General of Health Services today (15) confirmed 20 deaths that occurred from 7"
to 15 May, due to Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 941 by now.

01. The deceased is a 67 year old female resident in Panvila. She died on 11.05.2021 while
undergoing treatments in National Hospital Kandy. The cause of death is mentioned as
Covid pneumonia.

02. The deceased is a 60 year old male resident in Kelaniya. He died on 08.05.2021 on
admission to Base Hospital Kiribathgoda. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia Stroke and Diabetes.

03. The deceased is a 61 year old male resident in Bulathsinhala. He died on 12.05.2021 in
his residence and the cause of death is mentioned as Covid 19 chest infection and
complications, Diabetes and heart disease.

04. The deceased is an 87 year old female resident in Gonapola. She died on 13.05.2021 in
her residence and the cause of death is mentioned as severe Covid pneumonia.

05. The deceased is a 70 year old male resident in Gampaha. He died on 14.05.2021 while
undergoing treatments in Special Covid Treatment Centre Welikanda. The cause of
death is mentioned as severe Covid pneumonia.

06. The deceased is a 71 year old male resident in Kalawana. He was transferred from
Kalawana Hospital to the Base Hospital Pimbura where he died on 15.05.2021. The
cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

07. The deceased is a 73 year old male resident in Polonnaruwa. He died on 14.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in Special Covid Treatment Centre Welikanda. The cause
of death is mentioned as severe Covid pneumonia

08. The deceased is a 72 year old female resident in Ambathenna. She was transferred from
Katugasthotha Hospital to the National Hospital Kandy where she died on 15.05.2021
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

09. The deceased is a 65 year old female resident in Kundasale. She died on 14.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in National Hospital Kandy. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

10. The deceased is a 75 year old female resident in Kaluthara North. She died on
15.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in General Hospital Kalutara. The cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.
