Press Release

A short while ago, the Director General of Health Services confirmed two (02)
deaths caused by Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of
deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 142 by now.

01.The deceased is a 62 year old male resident of Colombo whose exact
residence has not been reported. He was transferred from General Hospital
Colombo to Base Hospital Homagama where he died on 07.12.2020. The
cause of death is Covid pneumonia.

02.The deceased is a 77 year old male from Jampata Street, He was transferred
from General Hospital Colombo to Base Hospital Homagama where he died
on 07.12.2020. The cause of death is blood poisoning with Covid-19 virus
infection and diabetes.
