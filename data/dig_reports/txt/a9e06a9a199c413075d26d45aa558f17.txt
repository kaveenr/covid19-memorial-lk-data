Press Release

A short while ago, the Director General of Health Services confirmed two (02)
deaths caused by Covid-19 virus infection and accordingly the total number of
deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 146 by now.

01.The deceased is a 66 year old female from Colombo 02. She was diagnosed
as infected with covid-19 virus and transferred to Base Hospital Homagama
from General Hospital Colombo. She died on 10.12.2020 and the cause of
death is heart failure due to Covid 19 virus infection.

02.The deceased is a 54 year old female from Weligamat, She has arrived from
Dubai and transferred from the quarantine centre to base hospital
Homagama where she died on 10.12.2020 at Intensive Care Unit. The cause
of death is exacerbation of kidney decease due to Covid-19 virus infection.
