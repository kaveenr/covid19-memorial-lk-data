Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 379 by now.

01.The deceased is an 83 year old male from Colombo 05. He died on 09.02.2021
while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Colombo. The Cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia, acute diabetes, high blood pressure
and Parkinson disease.

02. The deceased is a 70 year old female from Kotahena. She died on 08.02.2021
on admission to General Hospital, Colombo. The Cause of death is mentioned
as Covid pneumonia, high blood pressure, a heart disease and anemia.

03.The deceased is a 42 year old female from Colombo 13. She died on
10.02.2021 while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Colombo. The
Cause of death is mentioned as acute lung cancer with Covid 19, asthma, high
blood pressure and acute diabetes.

04.The deceased is a 64 year old female from Udathalawinna. She died on
02.02.2021 while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Kandy. The
cause of death is mentioned as blood poisoning shock with Covid pneumonia
and heart failure.
