Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed seven (07) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka
is 483 by now.

01. The deceased is an 87 year old female resident from Colombo 15. She died on 01.03.2021
at her residence. The Cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia, acute diabetes,
acute kidney disease and high blood pressure.

02. The deceased is an 89 year old female resident from Colombo 05. She died on 01.03.2021.
The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and acute diabetes.

03. The deceased is a 78 year old female resident from Colombo 15. She died on 02.03.2021
while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Colombo. The cause of death is mentioned
as Covid pneumonia, acute diabetes and high blood pressure.

04. The deceased is a 73 year old male resident form Piliandala. He was diagnosed as infected
with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Base Hospital Pimbura to Base Hospital
Homagama where he died on 01.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia and respiratory system failure.

05. The deceased is a 63 year old female resident from Pilimathalawa. She was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 and transferred from Teaching Hospital Peradeniya to Base Hospital
Theldeniya where she died on 02.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as acute Covid
pneumonia, High Blood pressure and acute diabetes.

06. The deceased is a 63 year old male resident from Colombo 02. He was diagnosed as infected
with Covid 19 and transferred from General Hospital Colombo to Teaching Hospital
Anuradhapura where he died on 01.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

07. The deceased is a 63 year old male from Galewela. He was diagnosed as infected with Covid
19 and transferred from District General Hospital Mathale to Base Hospital Theldentya
where he died on 02.03.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and a
lung cancer.
