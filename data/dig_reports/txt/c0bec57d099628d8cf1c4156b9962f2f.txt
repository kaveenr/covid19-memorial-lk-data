Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed two deaths caused by Covid-
19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 620 by now.

01. The deceased is a 58 year old female resident in Mahawa. She was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at
District Hospital Galgamuwa and transferred to Teaching Hospital
Anuradhapura where she died on 17.04.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as Severe Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome due to COVID
Pneumonia.

02. The deceased is an 84 year old male resident in Marassana. He was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at
National Hospital Kandy and transferred to Base Hospital Theldeniya
where he died on 19.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Severe
COVID Pneumonia in the background of Diabetes Mellitus,
Hypertension, Dyslipidaemia and Heart Failure.
