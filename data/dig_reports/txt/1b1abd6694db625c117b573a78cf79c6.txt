Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed two deaths caused by Covid-
19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 595 by now.

01. The deceased is a 66 year old female resident in Vavuniyawa. She was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at
District Hospital Vavuniyawa and transferred to Base Hospital
Homagama where she died on 08.04.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid 19 pneumonia and septic shock.

02. The deceased is a 71 year old male resident in Yalwela. He was diagnosed
as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at Base
Hospital Bibila and transferred to Base Hospital Mulleriyawa where he
died on 08.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia, heart disease and acute kidney disease, diabetes and high
blood pressure
