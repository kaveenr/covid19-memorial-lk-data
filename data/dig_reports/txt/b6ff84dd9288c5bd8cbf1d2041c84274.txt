Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection yesterday (02.04.2021). Accordingly, the total number
of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 575 by now.

1; The deceased is an 86 year old female resident in Ekiriya. She was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at
Base Hospital Rikillagaskada and transferred to Teaching Hospital Kandy
where she died on 01.04.2021. he cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.

2, The deceased is a 67 year old male resident in Homagama. He was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments at
Base Hospital Minuwangoda and transferred to Base Hospital
Mulleriyawa where he died on 01.04.2021. The cause of death is
mentioned as acute respiratory distress syndrome with Covid
pneumonia.

3. The deceased is an 87 year old male resident in Puwakpitiya. He was
diagnosed as infected with Covid 19 while undergoing treatments at Base
Hospital Awissawella and transferred to Base Hospital Mulleriyawa
where he died on 01.04.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as sepsis,
Covid pneumonia, high blood pressure and stroke.

4. The deceased is a 90 year old male resident in Colombo 14. He died on
31.03.2021 at his residence. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid
pneumonia.
