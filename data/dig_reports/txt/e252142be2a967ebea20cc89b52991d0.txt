Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed two deaths caused by Covid-
19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-
19 infection in Sri Lanka is 581 by now.

01. The deceased is a 79 year old female resident in Pansiyagama. She died on
02.04.2021 at her residence. The cause of death is mentioned as Ischemic
Heart Disease and COVID 19 infection.

02. The deceased is a 61 year old male resident in Moronthota. He died on
18.03.2021 while undergoing treatments at Teaching Hospital Kegalle.
The cause of death is mentioned as Congestive cardiac failure and chronic
obstructive pulmonary disease complicated by COVID 19 infection.
