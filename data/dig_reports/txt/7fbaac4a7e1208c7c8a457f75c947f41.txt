Twenty three (23) Covid Deaths Reported

The Director General of Health Services today confirmed 23 deaths that occurred from 05" to
uw" May, due to Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 850 by now.

1. The deceased is an 84 year old female resident in Rathmalana. She died on 08.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in a private Hospital in Colombo. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

2. The deceased is a 61 year old male resident in Polonnaruwa. He died on 11.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in Welikanda Treatment Centre. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

35 The deceased is a 70 year old female resident in Pilimathalawa. She died on
11.05.2021 while undergoing treatments in Teaching Hospital Peradeniya. The cause
of death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

4. The deceased is a 79 year old male resident in Pathana. He died on 08.05.2021 while
undergoing treatments in District General Hospital Nuwara Eliya. The cause of death
is mentioned as Covid infection.

5: The deceased is a 57 year old male resident in Thalawakele. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus while undergoing treatments in Lindula Divisional
Hospital and transferred to District General Hospital Nuwara Eliya where he died on
11.05.2021. The cause of death is mentioned as Covid 19 pneumonia and Diabetes.

6. The deceased is a 76 year old female resident in Halgranoya. She died on 09.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in District General Hospital Nuwara Eliya. The cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

7. The deceased is a 77 year old female resident in Mathale. She died on 07.05.2021
while undergoing treatments in District General Hospital Mathale. The cause of death
is mentioned as Chronic liver disease, Covid infection, and Sepsis.
