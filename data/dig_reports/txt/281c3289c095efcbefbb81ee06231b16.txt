The deceased is an 89 year old male from Nawala. He was transferred from Nevil Fernando
Hospital in Colombo to the IDH Hospital where he died on 04.02.2021. The Cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia, damaged kidneys due to acute kidney disease, blood
poisoning and a shock due to blood poisoning, chronic heart disease and acute diabetes.

The deceased is a 72 year old male from Bagawanthalawa. He was diagnosed as infected
with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Base Hospital Dikoya to the Teaching Hospital
Anuradhapura where he died on 04.02.2021. The Cause of death is mentioned as a heart
failure due to Covid pneumonia.

The deceased is a 63 year old female from Mattakkuliya. She was transferred from General
Hospital Colombo to the Base Hospital Homagama where she died on 04.02.2021. The
Cause of death is mentioned as shock due to blood poisoning and multiple organ dysfunction
caused by Covid pneumonia.
