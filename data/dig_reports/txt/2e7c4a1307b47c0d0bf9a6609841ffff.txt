Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed seven (07) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection today. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to
Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is 339 by now.

01.The deceased is a 58 year old male from Maggona. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from Colombo South Teaching
Hospital to the IDH Hospital where he died on 03.02.2021. The Cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and a complicated kidney disease.

02.The deceased is an 82 year old male from Weuda. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from a private hospital in Kandy
to the Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on 03.02.2021. The Cause of
death is mentioned as Covid pneumonia, an acute heart disease and damaged
kidneys.

03.The deceased is a 70 year old male from Katugasthota. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus and transferred from National Hospital Kandy to
the Base Hospital Theldeniya where he died on 03.02.2021. The Cause of
death is mentioned as a kidney disease with acute Covid pneumonia and heart
decease.

04.The deceased is a 53 year old male from Hettipola. He died on 04.02.2021
while undergoing treatments at Base Hospital Homagama. The cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia.

05.The deceased is a 66 year old male from Biyagama. He died on 02.02.2021
while undergoing treatments at General Hospital Colombo. The cause of death
is mentioned as Covid pneumonia and an acute lung infection.
