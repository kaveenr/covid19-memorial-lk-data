News Release

Dr. Anil Jasinghe, Director General of Health Services reported that two new Corona cases have
been confirmed so far today (13.05.2020) and the both patients are members of the Sri Lanka
Navy.

The number of patients completely recovered and discharged from hospitals is 382.

The number of Corona infected patients who are presently receiving treatments in hospitals is
500.

The total number of Corona patients reported in the country is 891 by now.

The number of PCR tests performed yesterday (12.05.2020) was 1078 and 20 patients were
identified.
