Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed six (06) deaths caused by Covid-19 virus
infection and accordingly the total number of deaths due to Covid-19 infection in Sri Lanka is
171 by now.

01. The deceased is a 39 year old female from Colombo 14. She was diagnosed as infected
with Covid-19 infection and transferred from the General Hospital Colombo to IDH
Hospital where she died on 19.12.2020. The cause of death is blood poisoning and
multiple organ failure and Covid pneumonia.

02. The deceased is a 68 year old male from Weeragula. He died on 18.12.2020 on admission
to base hospital Wathupitiwala. The cause of death is mentioned as chest infection caused
by Covid-19 virus infection.

03. The deceased is a 77 year old male from Colombo 15. He died on admission to the
General Hospital Colombo on 17.12.2020. The cause of death is high blood pressure,
diabetes and Covid pneumonia.

04. The deceased is a 76 year old male from Colombo 10. He died on admission to the
General Hospital Colombo on 17.12.2020. The cause of death is Covid pneumonia.

05. The deceased is an 88 year old female from Kiriwaththuduwa. She died at her residence
on 18.12.2020. The cause of death is a heart attack caused by high blood pressure and
chest infection due to Covid 19 virus.

06. The deceased is an 83 year old female from Bandaragama. She died at her residence on
18.12.2020. The cause of death is a heart attack caused by high blood pressure and chest
infection due to Covid 19 virus
