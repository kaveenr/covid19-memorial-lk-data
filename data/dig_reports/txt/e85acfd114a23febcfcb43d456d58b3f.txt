Press Release

The Director General of Health Services confirmed four (04) deaths caused by
Covid-19 virus infection. Accordingly, the total number of deaths due to Covid-19
infection in Sri Lanka is 199 by now.

01.The deceased is a 72 year old male from Batticaloa. He died on 30.12.2020
while being treated at Teaching Hospital Batticaloa. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia and meningitis.

02. The deceased is a 70 year old female from Colombo 10. She was diagnosed as
infected with Covid 19 virus at General Hospital Colombo and transferred to
Base Hospital Homagama where she died on 29.12.2020. The cause of death
is acute liver infection and stroke due to blood poisoning with Covid-19 virus
infection.

03.The deceased is a 50 year old male from Kolonnawa. He was diagnosed as
infected with Covid -19 virus and transferred from General Hospital Colombo
to Base Hospital Homagama where he died on 29.12.2020. The cause of death
is acute kidney infection with Covid-19 virus infection and a heart disease with
high blood pressure.

04. The deceased is a 66 year old male from Colombo 15. He died on admission
to the General Hospital Colombo on 29.12.2020. The cause of death is
mentioned as Covid pneumonia and acute diabetes.
